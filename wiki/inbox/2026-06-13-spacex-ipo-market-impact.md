### 1. Liquidity Absorption vs. Broad Lift (Index-Level)
Day-1 index reaction was positive but muted relative to event size (prior synthesis: S&P +0.5%, Nasdaq +0.3%, Dow +0.7%). Heavy primary raise ($75B) + retail/institutional demand (reports of 4x oversubscribed elements, staff 5% reserve, elevated retail allocation discussion) acted more as capital *absorber* than net injector. Pre-IPO private-market and prediction-market expectations had run hotter (~$2T+ valuations discussed); the $135 direct price and $150 open were below some "indicated" levels. Net: a sentiment-positive regime marker for narrative growth assets, but not a broad risk-on flood.

### 2. The Siphon Effect — Strongest Immediate Market Impact (Sector Proxies)
The dominant observed transmission channel was **capital and attention rotation out of listed space pure-plays into the newly investable marquee name** ("big fish" displacement).

- Pre-IPO / anticipation window: proxies ran (RKLB cited +6% premarket on 6/12, strong YTD runs including ~449% for RKLB per one analysis; ASTS, RDW, LUNR, PL, FLY also participated in "space TAM validator" re-rating).
- Post-open reversal (midday 6/12 data cited across X + reporting):
  - RKLB: down 10–17% (one track: round-tripped from ~$123 high to ~$103 low).
  - ASTS: down 10–14%.
  - RDW: down ~11%.
  - SPCE: down 24–37% (exacerbated by retail ticker confusion).
  - EchoStar (SATS, estimated small SpaceX stake): down ~13%.
- Interpretation (multiple independent observers): investors who had built "accessible" exposure while SPCX was private rotated into the real thing. The proxy premium that had built on anticipation "bled out." Classic sell-the-news on the sector level even as the flagship popped.
- Falsifiable hook (links to existing): exactly the test in [[../wiki/07_sector_handoff]] §3 — post-IPO 30-day **institutional flows** (13F / Finviz Inst Trans turning positive for space pool) vs. "利好出盡" pullback >30% + outflows = this was IPO hype rotation, not structural handoff. The siphon validates the "capital migrated to SPCX" clause in the June calendar.

This is a liquidity-fishbowl demonstration: finite marginal capital chasing a hot new narrative vehicle compresses the adjacent names on debut day.

- Structural implication: TSLA remains the nearer-term, more liquid Musk sentiment gauge for many; SPCX becomes the higher-beta "pure space + orbital AI" expression for new or incremental capital that could never access pre-IPO shares. Divergence between the two (or correlated crashes on negative Musk headlines) becomes a live monitoring signal.
