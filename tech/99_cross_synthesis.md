## В§3. ж–ҮеҢ– вҶ’ йңҖжұӮ вҶ’ дҫӣжҮүйҸҲ / Culture вҶ’ demand вҶ’ supply-chain map

The principal's instinct that *culture drives the tech* is correct and is the substrate under several trends ([[youth-culture-shifts]] is the hub):

- **е№ҙиј•дәәеҒҘеә· / GLP-1** вҶ’ the [[ai-pharma-glp1]] cash-flow engine; also the declining-alcohol shift (real, but health-perception + economics, not pure "lifestyle revolution" вҖ” narrative-inflated).

## В§5. иіӘи®Ҡ vs еҗҢжә«еұӨ вҖ” дё»зөҗи«– / Master read

1. **The cycle is real; the иіӘи®Ҡ is mostly already recognised.** 8 of 9 score A1=2 (real technical moat) and 9 of 9 score A3=2 (capital + authority all-in). That ubiquity of capital is a **late-cycle** signal, not a buy signal вҖ” it matches [[../wiki/07_ai_bubble_audit]]'s "1970s extended-bull, equity-ahead-of-fundamental" thesis.
2. **You are mostly buying зөҗж§Ӣ, not undiscovered иіӘи®Ҡ.** The alpha that remains is (a) the verifiable pricing-power chokepoint (В§4), and (b) the *second-derivative* nodes that aren't yet crowded.
3. **Two echo-chamber traps the data flags to avoid:** AI-edge-devices (**йҒҺзҶұ** вҖ” the AI-PC supercycle is a marketing wrapper; the real pull is memory) and quantum-breaks-BTC (**еӨӘж—©** вҖ” 105 physical qubits vs ~13M needed, Q-Day ~2033, BTC can soft-fork first). 4. **The one cash-flow иіӘи®Ҡ:** GLP-1 вҖ” realised P&L, not a promise. The AI-pharma half is a real-but-unrealised option that the market prices like cash flow (the FDA Phase-II/III gate is intact; zero AI-discovered NMEs approved).
5. **The single most important cross-cutting risk:** *the equity is ahead of the real fundamental* in memory, CPO, the model layer, and the software "winners" simultaneously. If the AI-capex first-derivative rolls over, the bottleneck stack (В§2 combo #1) cracks first вҖ” and HBM bit-demand is the leading tell ([[memory-supercycle]] вҶ’ [[../wiki/07_ai_bubble_audit]] В§6 tier-1 indicator #3).

- **йҒҝй–Ӣ Core / еғ… Moonshot ring-fence вүӨ5% (йҒҺзҶұ/еӨӘж—©/жҗ¶и·‘)**: йҮҸеӯҗ (IONQ/QBTS/RGTIпјҢеӨӘж—©)пјӣAI-PC и¶…зҙҡеҫӘз’°ж•ҳдәӢ ([[ai-edge-devices]] йҒҺзҶұ)пјӣPLTR/Anduril (е°Қзҡ„е‘ҪйЎҢгҖҒ>85Г— P/S жҗ¶и·‘)пјӣPop Mart еңЁ +184% й«ҳеҹәжңҹ (йҒҺеәҰж“Ғж“ )пјӣASTS (дәҢе…ғеӢҹиіҮиіӯжіЁ)пјӣCursor-as-equity (иІ жҜӣеҲ© wrapper + жңӘдёҠеёӮ)гҖӮ
- **зңҹжӯЈзҡ„ж—©жңҹйӮҠ = жңӘж“Ғж“ дәҢйҡҺзҜҖй»һ**: е…үдә’йҖЈ hybrid-bond **йҮҸжё¬** (Auros/Onto/FormFactor вҖ” [[optical-supply-chain-deep]]пјҢCPO ж•ҳдәӢеҝҪз•Ҙзҡ„зҜҖй»һ)пјӣHBM йҮҸжё¬пјӣGLP-1 API/fill-finish (дҪҶзӣҜ LLY/NVO иҮӘе»ә)пјӣAR еҫ®йЎҜзӨә/жіўе°ҺпјӣеҸ°зҒЈ satcom RF/жҝҫжіў/PCB дёҠжёёгҖӮ
