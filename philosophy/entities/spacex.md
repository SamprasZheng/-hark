# SpaceX (SPCX)

Listed 2026-06-12 on Nasdaq. First-day close $160.95 (+19.2% from IPO price $135). **Under 90-day post-IPO blackout per [[../06-exclusions]] — not eligible for any signal slot until 2026-10-08 (90 trading days).**

## One-line business model

Designs, manufactures and launches rockets (Falcon 9, Starship) and operates Starlink LEO satellite internet; xAI (Grok AI platform) was merged in at IPO, adding an AI revenue stream. Revenue is primarily Starlink subscriber fees (~61% of FY2025 ~$18.7B) plus launch services and government contracts (NASA, DoD).

## Why it matters to the system

- **Musk ecosystem proxy**: TSLA and SPCX share the same sentiment driver — Elon Musk news, policy shifts (DOGE, tariffs), social-media cycles. A sharp SPCX move is a leading indicator of TSLA sentiment rotation.
- **Nasdaq mega-cap event**: SpaceX IPO was the largest US IPO in history ($75B raised, exceeding Saudi Aramco's 2019 ~$29B). Its inclusion reshapes Nasdaq index composition and could affect QQQ/XLK weightings.
- **AI convergence signal**: xAI merger + Starlink orbital data-centers thesis positions SPCX as an AI infrastructure play adjacent to the AI supply-chain tracked in [[../concepts/supply-chain-bottleneck]]. If the thesis gains traction, it competes for the same capital pool as MSFT/GOOGL/AMZN hyperscaler AI capex names.
- **Competitor pressure gauge**: RKLB (Rocket Lab) and other pure-play space names face direct capital-displacement from SPCX. Monitor correlation inversion.

## Bottleneck / vulnerability map

- **Starship execution**: Starship full-operational status is the single largest valuation optionality item and the largest execution risk. Slippage = multi-year revenue model revision.
- **FAA launch cadence**: regulatory approval rate caps revenue scaling. Geopolitical (Starlink in conflict zones) adds headline risk.
- **Profitability path**: current operating loss means valuation is 100% forward-story. Any guidance cut or macro re-rate would compress multiples sharply.
- **AI narrative dependency**: xAI / Grok-based revenue is unproven. If Grok loses share to OpenAI / Gemini / Claude, the AI premium evaporates.
- **Musk sentiment risk**: TSLA has demonstrated that a single Musk tweet / regulatory event can swing ±20% in days. SPCX inherits this. No hedged short position allowed (high-short-interest rule applies per [[../03-long-short-taxonomy]] once the blackout lifts).
- **Lock-up expiry**: employee lock-up release (typically 6–12 months post-IPO) creates future supply overhang. Watch 2026-12 / 2027-06 windows.
- **Index inclusion**: S&P 500 inclusion requires profitability; SPCX is currently loss-making, so near-term S&P inclusion is **unlikely**. Nasdaq-100 (QQQ) inclusion requires meeting profitability and float criteria.
