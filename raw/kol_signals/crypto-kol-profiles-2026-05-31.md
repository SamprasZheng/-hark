# Crypto / Web3 KOL profiles — 底層邏輯性格

## The one lesson that survives all of them

Every figure below is **structurally long and monetizes your engagement** — via their own
token/treasury, a fund book, a paid newsletter, or ad/sub revenue. **Winners get
screenshotted; losers get memory-holed.** Use their *frameworks* (liquidity, on-chain,
risk-bands, cycle structure) as reading lenses; treat every *price target and named-token
call as zero-information*; and **never size a position off a tweet.** This is the same
narrative-seduction that produced the principal's ~400萬 loss — the tracker exists to watch
it coldly, not to obey it.

**Grade key:** A/B reserved for verifiable EVENTS (a policy action, an 8-K, a protocol
fact) — never sentiment. C = transparent data-driven analytical input (framing only).
D = sentiment/narrative. E = falsified model or documented grift (reverse-indicator only).
